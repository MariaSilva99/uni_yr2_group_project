Art Auction Event

This art auction event will be taking place in Birmingham at the Museum and Art Gallery and it is to raise fund for the homeless people. All the fund will be delivered to the St.Anne's Hostel in Birmingham so that we can bring back the smile to all the people that have no where to go.

The address is Chamberlain Square, Birmingham B3 3DH

Date and Time: at 13:00 pm on the 10/06/2021
