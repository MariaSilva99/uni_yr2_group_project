CYCLING EVENT

This cycling event will be taking place in Manchester and it is to raise fund for all the cancer patient. All the fund will be delivered to the Royal Manchester Hospital so that we can bring back the smile to all the patient affected. The cycling start line will be at the university of Manchester and it will end at the Royal Manchester Hospital.

The address is Oxford Rd, Manchester M13 9PL

Date and Time: at 13:00 pm on the 01/07/2021
